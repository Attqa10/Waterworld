Welcome to Sanjalika Water Park

Discover the largest water park in the region, filled with thrilling rides, fun-filled slides, and exciting attractions for visitors of all ages. Whether you’re looking for an adrenaline rush or a relaxing experience, we have something for everyone!

Main Attractions

The Giant Cave

This one-of-a-kind attraction is designed for thrill-seekers, families, and anyone who loves the mysterious allure of underground exploration. The Giant Cave is not just a water-based attraction—it's an entire immersive experience. As you step inside, you’ll leave the outside world behind and enter a realm of cool, sparkling waters, twisting tunnels, and unexpected surprises. Here’s everything you need to know about this thrilling water cave adventure!

Wave Pool

Experience the thrill of the ocean right in the heart of our water park! Our Massive Wave Pool offers the ultimate beach-like experience for visitors of all ages. Whether you’re a beginner testing the waters or an experienced swimmer looking for a challenge, the Wave Pool has something for everyone. It’s one of our most popular attractions, drawing guests from all over for the chance to ride the waves in a safe and exciting environment. Our History

Sanjalika Water Park opened its doors in 2010, and since then, it has become a top destination for families and adventure enthusiasts. The park is known for its unique blend of thrilling rides and family-friendly attractions, making it the perfect place to create lasting memories.
