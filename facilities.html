Our Facilities

At Sanjalika Water Park, we provide a variety of facilities to make your visit comfortable and enjoyable. Explore the facilities available to all our visitors.

Changing Rooms & Lockers

Changing Rooms

We offer clean and spacious changing rooms for both men and women, ensuring a hassle-free experience for all guests.

Lockers

Secure lockers are available to store your belongings while you enjoy the rides and slides.

First Aid & Safety

First Aid Stations

Our first aid stations are strategically located throughout the park, offering immediate care if needed.

Lifeguards on Duty

We have trained lifeguards stationed at all water attractions to ensure your safety throughout your visit.

Free Wi-Fi & Charging Stations

Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected during your visit with our free Wi-Fi available throughout the park.

Charging Stations

Need to charge your phone? Our charging stations are available at key locations around the park.

Parking & Transport

Parking

We offer ample parking space for visitors, making it easy for you to access the park and start your adventure.

Shuttle Service

Our convenient shuttle service ensures smooth transport between the parking area and the park entrance.
